## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 3, 2026

Repryl ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Community Hub extension ("Extension") for Azure DevOps.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### Configuration Preferences

The Extension stores minimal configuration preferences to personalize your experience:

- **Category Settings:** Custom category configurations for organizing discussions (names, colors, icons)
- **Field Mappings:** Work item field mappings used to store discussion data
- **User Preferences:** Display preferences such as theme settings

These preferences are stored within Azure DevOps using the Extension Data Service and are scoped to your organization or project. They are not transmitted outside of Azure DevOps.

#### Usage Analytics

We collect anonymized usage analytics to improve the Extension. This data is collected via **Aptabase**, a privacy-focused analytics service hosted in the European Union.

**Events we track include:**

- Application startup and page views
- Feature usage (e.g., creating discussions, adding comments, voting)
- Error occurrences (error type only, no details)

**What we do NOT collect:**

- Personal information (names, emails, user IDs)
- Discussion content, comments, or titles
- Organization names or project data
- Azure DevOps credentials or tokens

### 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Configuration Preferences:** Provide a personalized experience within the Extension
- **Usage Analytics:** Understand how the Extension is used, identify popular features, diagnose issues, and guide future development

### 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Configuration Preferences:** Stored securely within Azure DevOps Extension Data Service, scoped to your organization or project. Data is retained as long as you have the Extension installed.
- **Usage Analytics:** Processed and stored by Aptabase in the European Union. Aptabase is designed with privacy in mind and does not use cookies or collect personally identifiable information.

### 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties.

- **Aptabase:** Receives anonymized usage analytics only. No personally identifiable information is shared.
- **Azure DevOps:** Configuration preferences are stored within your Azure DevOps environment and are subject to Microsoft's privacy practices.
